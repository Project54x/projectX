1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve user experience, remember preferences, and analyze site performance.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – Required for website functionality.

  • Performance Cookies – Help us analyze site performance (e.g., Google Analytics).

  • Functional Cookies – Store preferences like language settings.

  • Marketing Cookies – Used for tracking and advertising (if applicable).

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure website functionality.

  • Improve user experience.

  • Track and analyze visitor behavior for website optimization.


We do not use cookies for targeted advertising.

4. Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can manage cookies through browser settings:

Chrome: [Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and other site data]
Safari: [Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data]

Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.
